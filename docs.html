AuraStar Documentation

Status: Early-stage community and concept

What AuraStar Is

AuraStar is a community-first project exploring identity, meaning, and coordination through zodiac archetypes. It treats astrology as a cultural framework — not belief, not finance.

What AuraStar Is Not

  • Not a token (yet)
  • Not financial advice
  • Not a speculative scheme

Principles

  • Clarity over hype
  • Transparency over promises
  • Execution over speculation

Roadmap (High Level)

  • Phase 1 — Community & narrative foundation
  • Phase 2 — Content, engagement, and participation loops
  • Phase 3 — Utility exploration (only if demand exists)
